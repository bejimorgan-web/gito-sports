# Product Vision

GiTO Live Sports should feel like a modern sports operations platform: fast, structured, reliable, and calm under pressure.

## Vision

Give sports operators a clean control center for turning scheduled matches into approved, watchable mobile streams.

## Target Users

- Sports streaming operators.
- Competition organizers.
- Technical broadcast coordinators.
- Mobile viewers who need reliable access to approved live matches.

## Product Values

- Reliability over novelty.
- Clear approvals over ambiguous publication.
- Sports taxonomy that can grow beyond football.
- Professional operator UX with low cognitive load.
- Modular architecture that remains understandable.
